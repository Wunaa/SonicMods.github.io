About the Mod

Sonic x Sonic Generations is a passion project aimed at reimagining Shadow’s adventure through the lens of Sonic. The mod replaces Shadow with Sonic, complete with custom animations, new character models, and fully integrated gameplay and cutscene mechanics. From the fast-paced platforming to perfectly synchronized animations, this mod offers a fresh, dynamic experience for fans.

Our Story

It all began with a simple question: *What if Sonic took over Shadow’s role?* This idea sparked a team of modders to come together and build something unique. Through countless hours of collaboration, troubleshooting, and creativity, Sonic x Sonic Generations was born. What started as a small experiment has grown into a complete mod that offers an immersive experience from start to finish.

Our Vision

We aim to give Sonic fans a fresh perspective on the Sonic universe, combining nostalgia with exciting new elements. This mod isn’t just about swapping characters—it’s about creating a unique experience that feels authentic to both Sonic and Shadow fans alike.
