About Sonic x Sonic Generations

Discover the journey, meet the team, and see what’s next for this exciting mod!

What is this Mod?

This mod reimagines Shadow’s role by putting Sonic front and center. With custom animations, new gameplay mechanics, and full integration into cutscenes, Sonic x Sonic Generations delivers an entirely new experience for fans.

Our Story

It all started with a simple idea: *What if Sonic replaced Shadow?* That spark led to a passion project that has grown into a full-fledged mod. What began as a fun experiment now brings a new experience to the Sonic community, blending nostalgia with exciting new elements.

Our Vision

We want to give Sonic fans something fresh and thrilling. This mod isn't just a character swap—it’s a celebration of Sonic’s legacy in a whole new light!
