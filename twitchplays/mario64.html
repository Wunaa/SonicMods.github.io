Mario 64 Controls

Use these commands to control Mario in the game!

  • Forward - Move forward
  • Run - Move forward for 3 seconds
  • Back - Move backwards
  • Jump - Jump
  • Punch - Punch
  • Crouch - Crouch
  • Fump - Jump forward
  • Rump - Jump right
  • Bump - Jump backwards
  • Lump - Jump left
  • Forward Left - Move forward and left
  • Forward Right - Move forward and right
  • Back Left - Move backwards and left
  • Back Right - Move backwards and right
  • Triple Jump - Do a triple jump
  • Backflip - Do a backflip
  • Longjump - Perform a long jump
